The Problem: Understanding the User

The Erovenus community lacked authentic voices and deep engagement, making it difficult to build trust and understand evolving user needs in a sensitive market.

User Personas

The Curious Newcomer

"I want to learn but I'm not sure where to start and who to trust."

  • Seeks trustworthy information
  • Values privacy and discretion
  • Needs guidance

The Passionate Advocate

"I have experiences and opinions I want to share with others who understand."

  • Wants to share experiences
  • Seeks community belonging

Key Learnings

This project taught me that the most powerful marketing is rooted in user research. By listening first, I was able to create content that truly resonated, moving from broadcasting to building a dialogue.

The experience reinforced the importance of authenticity in community building, especially in sensitive markets. Trust is built through consistent, genuine engagement and by giving users a platform to share their own stories.
