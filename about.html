About Me

My journey from entrepreneurship to user experience design

My Story

My path to UX wasn't linear. It began by building a brand from the ground up at Lan Studio, where I learned business strategy and the customer journey firsthand. It deepened at Erovenus, where I moved from marketing to users to designing with them, conducting intimate research to understand their needs.

This passion for user-centric problem-solving led me to formalize my skills through the Google UX Certificate, where I applied a rigorous design process to create impactful digital products like the Museum and Clinic apps. I believe in designing experiences that are not only beautiful but also meaningful and effective.
