About Me

My path to UX wasn't linear. It began by building a brand from the ground up at Lan Studio, where I learned business strategy and the customer journey firsthand. It deepened at Erovenus, where I moved from marketing to users to designing with them, conducting intimate research to understand their needs. This passion for user-centric problem-solving led me to formalize my skills through the Google UX Certificate, where I applied a rigorous design process to create impactful digital products.

Approach

My process centers on listening — synthesizing insights into clear priorities, designing with constraints in mind, and validating through lightweight prototypes and user feedback.
