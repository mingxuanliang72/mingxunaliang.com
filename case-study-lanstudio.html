Key Learnings

Lan Studio was my first lesson in product-market fit. It ingrained in me the necessity of validating assumptions directly with users and using their feedback to iterate on everything from product descriptions to the entire purchase funnel.

The experience taught me how to balance business objectives with user needs, and how to build a brand that people connect with on an emotional level. This foundation in entrepreneurship continues to inform my approach to UX design and product strategy.
